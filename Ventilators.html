Two Main Modes of Mechanical Ventilations

  • A/C MV - Assist/Controlled Mechanical Ventilation
  • SIMV - Synchronized Intermittent Mechanical Ventilation

Controlled Ventilations

Volume Controlled

Same amount of tidal volume each breath

Pressure Controlled

Will ventilate until it reaches set pressure (tidal volume will vary)

Ventilator Settings

  • FiO2 - Percentage of Oxygen (Room air is 21%)
    • 100% is from the bottle
    • 50% is 21% from the air and rest from the bottle
  • Ptrig - Pressure needed to Trigger Breath
  • PEEP - What is PEEP?
    • Positive end-expiratory pressure
  • Airway Pressure (Paw) - Backpressure in the airway (everyone is different)
  • ƒ - frequency, how many breaths they take
  • Ti - inhalation time, how many seconds does your inhalation take
  • I:E Ratio - Inhalation/Exhalation Ratio (1:2 = your exhale is 2 as long as inhale)
    • If you take 2 seconds to breath in then it will take you 4 seconds to breath out
    • Effected by Frequency and Ti
  • Psupport - Pressure support, when using BiPAP, how much extra pressure is used during inhale
